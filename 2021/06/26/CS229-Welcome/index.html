Definition of Machine Learning

机器学习的定义最开始是在1959年由Arthur Samuel提出的，其内容为：“Machine Learning is the field of study that gives the computer the ability to learn without being explicitly programmed.” 此外Tom Mitchell在1998年认为：“a computer program is said to learn from experience E with respect to some class of tasks T and performance measure P, if its performance at tasks in T, as measured by P, improves with experience E.”而这是绝大多数人接受的观点
